Stammer [2008] comes to the conclusion that sea level rise from melting Greenland ice will have little effect on sea levels in the Pacific and other distant areas for over 50 years. This result has important ramifications and has been reported in the press. He derives a similar result for melting of Antarctica. However, these are conclusions that the paper is unable to support. A retraction needs to be printed.

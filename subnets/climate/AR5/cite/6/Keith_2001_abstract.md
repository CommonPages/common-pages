Concept. Fertilizing the 'biological pump' may enhance the flux of carbon into the oceans that maintains the disequilibrium in CO2 concentration between the atmosphere and the deep ocean. While use of nitrogen and phosphorus has been proposed, iron fertilization is the salient possibility because the ratio of iron addition to carbon fixation is very large (the Fe:C ratio is ∼1:104 whereas for N:C it is ∼1:6).

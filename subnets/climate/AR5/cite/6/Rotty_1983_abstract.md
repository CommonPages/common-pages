This paper revises earlier published data on the annual amounts of carbon released to the atmosphere during the period 1950â€“1978 and updates the record through 1980. A latitudinal distribution of the fossil fuel source is presented as an aid in explaining the differences in the observed CO2 concentrations at several stations.

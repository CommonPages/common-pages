Assessing stratosphere–troposphere coupling in observational data or model output requires a multi‐level index with high time resolution. Ideally, such an index would (1) represent spatial patterns in the troposphere that are most strongly coupled with stratospheric variability and (2) be robust and computationally feasible in both observations and standard model output.

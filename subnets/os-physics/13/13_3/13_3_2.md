The ideal gas law can be considered to be another manifestation of the law of conservation of energy (see <link:>). Work done on a gas results in an increase in its energy, increasing pressure and/or temperature, or decreasing volume. This increased energy can also be viewed as increased internal kinetic energy, given the gas’s atoms and molecules.
